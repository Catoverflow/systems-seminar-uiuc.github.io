UIUC Systems Verification Reading Group is a weekly meetup for anyone interested in systems verification research at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

We discuss recent significant work (e.g., published at top-tier conferences) and identify new research directions/ideas.

Join us on Slack: uiuc-sysnet.slack.com and search for channel #systems-verification-seminar

This seminar does not come with any credit.
